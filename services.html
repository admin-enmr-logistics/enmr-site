Services

ENMR Logistics offers freight coordination services for businesses shipping internationally and within the United States. Our focus is on helping clients manage the logistics process clearly and efficiently — without needing to coordinate every piece themselves.

We are not a licensed freight forwarder or customs broker (yet), but we work closely with trusted professionals who are. Our role is to handle communication, scheduling, and follow-up between all parties involved in a shipment.

We can assist with:

  • Ocean import coordination through licensed partners
  • Coordination of air freight from pickup to delivery
  • Communication with U.S. customs brokers for ISF and entry filings
  • Final-mile trucking and drayage at major ports
  • Quoting across multiple lanes or transport modes

If you’re not sure whether we’re the right fit for your shipment, just ask. If we can’t help directly, we’ll point you in the right direction.
